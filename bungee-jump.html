Tour Overview

Experience the ultimate thrill of bungee jumping in Pokhara, where adventure meets breathtaking natural beauty. Leap off the iconic platform, free-falling through the air as the stunning Himalayan mountains and lush valleys surround you. This tour is designed for both first-time jumpers and seasoned thrill-seekers seeking an unforgettable adrenaline rush.

Feel the rush of wind against your face as you descend toward the sparkling river below, with every heartbeat matched by the spectacular scenery. Our certified jump masters ensure a safe and exhilarating experience, allowing you to focus on the pure excitement of the jump while capturing memories that will last a lifetime.

Our certified jump masters ensure a safe, unforgettable experience for both first-timers and thrill-seekers. Capture every heartbeat with optional photo and video packages.
