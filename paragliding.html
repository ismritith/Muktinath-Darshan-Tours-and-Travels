Tour Overview

Soar high above the majestic Pokhara Valley and feel the ultimate thrill of paragliding, combined with the serene spiritual journey to Muktinath. This tour blends adventure and devotion, giving you a unique experience of Nepal’s natural beauty and sacred heritage.

From breathtaking flights over Phewa Lake and the Annapurna range to visits to Muktinath Temple, this journey promises unforgettable memories for adventure seekers and spiritual travelers alike.

Experience the exhilaration of flight as you glide with the wind, taking in panoramic views of the Annapurna range, lush valleys, and sparkling lakes below. Each tandem paragliding flight is designed to be safe yet thrilling, allowing you to feel the freedom of soaring like a bird while capturing memories that will last a lifetime. This adventure is perfect for both first-timers and experienced flyers seeking an unforgettable aerial experience in one of the world’s most stunning landscapes.
