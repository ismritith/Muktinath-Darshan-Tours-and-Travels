Tour Overview

Rise gently above Pokhara in a hot air balloon and experience a serene, unforgettable journey through the skies. Drift over lush valleys, sparkling lakes, and panoramic views of the Annapurna range as the world below unfolds like a painting.

Enjoy a peaceful flight at sunrise or sunset, capturing breathtaking vistas of Phewa Lake, Sarangkot, and the Himalayan peaks. Each balloon ride is guided by experienced pilots, ensuring a safe, smooth, and magical adventure for all passengers.

Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion, seeking incredible photography opportunities, or simply wanting a calm and awe-inspiring way to experience Pokhara, the hot air balloon tour offers a once-in-a-lifetime perspective of Nepal’s natural beauty. Perfect for couples, families, and travelers of all ages.
