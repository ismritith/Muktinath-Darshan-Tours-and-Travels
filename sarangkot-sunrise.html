Tour Overview

Wake up early and witness the breathtaking sunrise from Sarangkot, one of Pokhara’s most famous viewpoints. As the first light of day kisses the snow-capped peaks of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges, you’ll experience a truly magical and serene moment above the valley.

Enjoy panoramic views of Pokhara city, Phewa Lake, and the surrounding hills as the golden sun illuminates the landscape. This tour combines peaceful nature observation with the perfect photo opportunities, making it ideal for both photographers and travelers seeking tranquility and awe.

Whether you’re a sunrise chaser, a nature lover, or simply looking for a quiet escape from the city, the Sarangkot Sunrise tour promises unforgettable memories and a rejuvenating start to your day amidst some of Nepal’s most spectacular scenery.

Tour Highlights

  • Sunrise over Annapurna & Dhaulagiri
  • Panoramic Views of Pokhara Valley
  • Ideal for Photography & Nature Lovers
