Tour Overview

Take to the skies with our Heli Service and witness the majestic beauty of Pokhara and the Annapurna range from above. Enjoy breathtaking aerial views of snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and sparkling lakes, all from the comfort of a modern helicopter.

Fly over iconic landscapes like Phewa Lake, Sarangkot, and the Annapurna massif, capturing panoramic vistas that are impossible to experience from the ground. Each flight is guided by experienced pilots who ensure a safe and unforgettable journey.

Whether you’re seeking adventure, photography opportunities, or a serene way to experience Nepal’s natural wonders, our helicopter tours provide a unique perspective that combines thrill with tranquility. Perfect for first-time visitors, families, or anyone wanting to explore Pokhara’s landscapes from the sky.
