Tour Overview

Embark on a spiritual journey to the sacred Muktinath Temple, nestled at the foot of the Himalayas. This tour combines serene natural landscapes with the profound experience of visiting one of Nepal’s most revered pilgrimage sites.

Travel through picturesque valleys, traditional villages, and breathtaking mountain scenery as you make your way to Muktinath. Along the journey, enjoy the tranquility of nature, the warmth of local culture, and the opportunity to reflect and rejuvenate your spirit.

Whether you seek spiritual fulfillment, cultural exploration, or simply the beauty of Nepal’s highlands, the Muktinath package provides a unique and unforgettable experience. With guided support, comfortable travel, and plenty of time for reflection and photography, this journey is ideal for both first-time pilgrims and seasoned travelers.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Pokhara

Arrive in Pokhara, settle into your hotel, and enjoy a welcome dinner. Take some time to relax and prepare for the journey ahead to Muktinath.

Hotel Crystal Fall Welcome Dinner
Day 2

Drive to Jomsom

Travel by vehicle to Jomsom, passing through stunning landscapes of the Kali Gandaki Valley. Enjoy scenic views of mountains, rivers, and traditional villages along the way.

Guesthouse in Jomsom Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 3

Muktinath Temple Visit

Reach Muktinath Temple, a sacred site for both Hindus and Buddhists. Explore the temple, witness the 108 water spouts, and participate in local rituals while soaking in the Himalayan scenery.

Guesthouse in Muktinath Breakfast, Lunch
Day 4

Return to Jomsom & Sightseeing

Travel back to Jomsom, stopping at scenic viewpoints for photography. Visit local villages and markets, experiencing the culture and traditions of the Mustang region.

Guesthouse in Jomsom Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 5

Drive Back to Pokhara

Return to Pokhara via scenic mountain roads, enjoying final glimpses of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges. Evening at leisure in Pokhara.

Hotel Crystal Fall Breakfast, Lunch
Day 6

Leisure & Optional Sightseeing

Spend the day relaxing in Pokhara or book optional activities such as boating on Phewa Lake, visiting caves, or exploring Sarangkot for panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Hotel Crystal Fall Breakfast, Farewell Dinner
Day 7

Departure

Check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport or bus station for your onward journey. Take with you unforgettable memories of the Muktinath pilgrimage and the Mustang region.

N/A Breakfast
