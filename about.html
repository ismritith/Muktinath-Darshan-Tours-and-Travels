Our Story

Where Passion Meets Purpose

What began as humble treks and pilgrimages has grown into a full-fledged travel service offering adventure tours, heli rides, trekking, river rafting, paragliding, and spiritual journeys like Muktinath Darshan.

Every expedition we design is immersive, authentic, and safe. We combine local knowledge with personalized service, ensuring you witness Nepal’s majestic mountains, serene lakes, vibrant culture, and sacred sites — all while leaving a positive impact on the communities we visit.

Our Vision

To be Nepal’s leading travel and adventure company, delivering experiences that inspire, thrill, and connect travelers with the heart of the Himalayas.

Our Journey So Far

Key milestones that shaped Muktinath Darshan Tours

2015

Humble Beginnings

Started as a small team offering spiritual tours to Muktinath and nearby temples, sharing Nepal’s sacred heritage with travelers seeking meaningful experiences.

2018

Expanding Horizons

Introduced adventure and scenic tours across Pokhara Valley and the Annapurna region, blending spirituality with trekking, paragliding, and nature experiences.

2021

Community Impact

Became a preferred choice for both domestic and international travelers, with hundreds of happy clients exploring Nepal’s cultural and natural wonders safely and comfortably.

2025

Looking Ahead

Planning to expand spiritual and adventure packages across Nepal while embracing sustainable tourism, offering unforgettable journeys that honor culture and nature.
