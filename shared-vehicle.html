Tour Overview

Enjoy a comfortable and budget-friendly journey with our Shared Vehicle Service, designed for travelers who value convenience, safety, and affordability. Whether you're traveling to popular destinations or exploring scenic routes around Pokhara and beyond, our shared transport option ensures a smooth and reliable ride with fellow travelers.

Travel through Nepal’s breathtaking landscapes while relaxing in well-maintained vehicles driven by experienced and professional drivers. Our shared service is perfect for solo travelers, small groups, and backpackers who want a cost-effective way to reach their destination without compromising on comfort and safety.

With scheduled departures, organized seating, and friendly service, our shared vehicle option offers both efficiency and flexibility.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Pokhara & Transfer

Arrive in Pokhara and meet your fellow travelers at the pick-up point. Board the shared vehicle and transfer to your hotel. Spend the afternoon relaxing by Lakeside or exploring the local market.

Hotel Crystal Fall Welcome Dinner
Day 2

Shared Vehicle to Adventure Base

After breakfast, depart in your shared vehicle to the adventure starting point. Meet your guides, get safety instructions, and enjoy a short warm-up session. Spend the night at a nearby lodge or camp with your group.

Riverside Lodge Breakfast, Dinner
Day 3

Scenic Drive & Local Exploration

Board the shared vehicle to visit nearby scenic spots and villages. Enjoy photo stops and short hikes. Meet locals and learn about cultural highlights along the route.

Riverside Lodge Breakfast, Lunch
Day 4

Full-Day Shared Vehicle Excursion

Travel in the shared vehicle to a full-day adventure area. Stop at viewpoints, enjoy local snacks, and take part in optional short activities. Evening return to the lodge for relaxation.

Riverside Lodge Breakfast, Lunch
Day 5

Adventure & Team Activities

Spend the day engaging in group activities organized by your guide. Participate in team challenges, enjoy riverside games, or explore hidden trails. Evening bonfire and social time with your group.

Riverside Lodge Breakfast, Dinner
Day 6

Return Journey to Pokhara

After breakfast, board the shared vehicle for the return journey to Pokhara. Stop for sightseeing along the way and lunch at a local restaurant. Evening free to explore Lakeside or relax at your hotel.

Hotel Crystal Fall Breakfast, Lunch
Day 7

Departure Day

Check out from your hotel after breakfast and transfer via shared vehicle to the airport or bus station for your onward journey.

Hotel Crystal Fall Breakfast

What's Included

  • 2 nights accommodation in Pokhara
  • 4 nights accommodation during shared vehicle tour
  • Daily breakfast at the hotel/lodge
  • Shared vehicle transportation as per itinerary
  • Pick-up and drop-off at designated points
  • Experienced local tour guide for the entire journey
  • Sightseeing and photo stops along the route
  • All applicable road taxes and tolls
  • Welcome orientation and briefing
  • Group activities and optional local experiences included in itinerary

What's Not Included

  • International flights to Nepal
  • Lunch and dinner (except those specified in itinerary)
  • Personal expenses and souvenirs
  • Travel insurance (strongly recommended)
  • Tips for guides and drivers
  • Optional activities not mentioned in the itinerary
  • Alcoholic beverages and soft drinks
  • Single supplement for solo travelers if requested
  • Any damage caused to equipment or vehicle due to negligence
  • Government or local entry fees not mentioned in itinerary
